About me

I am currently a graduate student in the Department of Astronomy & Astrophysics at the University of California, Santa Cruz (UCSC). My research is mainly focusing on the formation and evolution of ultra-diffuse galaxies (UDG) in the local universe and galaxy clusters. Before I moved to the US, I got my Bachelor's degree in Astronomy at the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2021 summer.
